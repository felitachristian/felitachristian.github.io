Product Description

Life Begins At The Hop is a single by XTC released in 1979, reaching number no.44 on the UK singles chart, and it was included as a bbonus track on the re-releases of Drums and Wires. Their fifth single was the first indication of a change in their late-seventies sound from urgent post-punk to power pop. The song title references the 50's "rock and roll classic". The song itself carries a rock and roll influence.
